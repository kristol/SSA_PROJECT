KIDOUBLESA

"kidoublesa" (pronounced like “ki-double-s-a”) started to make music in 2018. Since the day he started, he has fallen in love with the process of making music. After he realised that he found the way how he could share his vision with the world, he decided to put more work into it, more than he ever did into anything. In the interest of making his music truly reflect his feelings, he will not spare his free time. He really pours hours into both the sound design and the lyrics in hopes to be someone you can relate to, without the help of outsiders such as sound engineers and producers or writers. Despite the fact that the most of us look at music as an industry only for entertainment, he sees it as a form of art of self expression as well.
